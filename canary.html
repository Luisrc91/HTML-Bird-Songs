While Canaries aren't really "hands on" birds like budgies or cockatiels,
they can be the perfect pet for someone who would like a smaller feathered friend for a companion.
While the Canary's small stature may make them seem like good pets for kids,
please be cautioned -- these birds are very fragile and easily frightened, and may not do well in a home with very young children.
